MESSES IN CAMPUS

There are TWO messes in the campus:

1. UG MESS
- Only students residing in the UG Residence is allowed in the UG Mess.
2. PG MESS
- Only students residing in the PG Residence is allowed in the PG Mess.

The students of their respective hostels are allowed in their respective mess. NO student is allowed in messes other than their. IF FOUND IN OTHER MESSES, fine will be charge and immediate action will be taken against the student.
