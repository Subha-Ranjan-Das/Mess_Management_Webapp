Regarding Applications

Regarding the Applications submissions, one has to submit them to the head office. The Head Office will take the responsibility to check whether you qualified for the accomodation into the hostels. The Services of Mess will be offered ones all the processes regarding the accomodations have been done.

The given information below is only applicable for those who have already fulfilled the above criteria. Those who have NOT FULFILLED the above criteria, please clear through all the process before applying for the accomodation.
