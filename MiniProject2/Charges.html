MESS CHARGES

BASIC CHARGES

The Mess Charges includes the commodities eaten on the regular basis. These includes Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

The Daily Charges for Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner:

  • 1. Breakfast = Rs. 40/-
  • 2. Lunch = Rs. 80/-
  • 3. Dinner = Rs. 75/-
    • * The Dinner on Wednesday and Sunday is of Rs 110/-.
    * The Student can choose their dinner from the Carte Menu given in Information on Foods in the homepage.

    MESS SCHEMES

    1. 30 Breakfasts and 30 Lunches- Rs.3600/-
    A student who will apply for this scheme will be granted Breakfast and Lunches for a whole month. After the end of the month, he/she will have to pay again for the same scheme or can take a different scheme.

    2. 30 Breakfasts and 15 Lunches- Rs.3000/-
    A student who will apply for this scheme will be granted 15 Breakfasts and Lunches for a whole month. After the end of the month, he/she will have to pay again for the same scheme or can take a different scheme.

    ADDITIONAL POINTS ABOUT MESS STRUCTURE

  • A student has to pre-book the Breakfast and Lunch as to prevent the wastage of food.
  • A student has to notify before leaving the Mess.
  • A student has to book the current day's dinner in the current day's lunch.
  • Student can also the other food services provided by Mess other the daily dinner by booking in the correct interval of time provided by the mess.
  • Extra money will be taken by the for packaging of food.
  • Any misconduct will be taken into serious consideration.
  • The student who have conducted the misconduct will have action taken against him/her. This may include Fine as well as he/she will NOT be given entry into the Mess.
