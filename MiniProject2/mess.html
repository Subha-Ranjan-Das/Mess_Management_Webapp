Mess Management System

This is the home page of Mess Management System website. Information about hostels, messes, rules and regulations, committees and other facilities at IISc hostel are available at this Website.

About

Mess Management System is a fully operational mess system. This system contains features such as information about food, committees responsible the management of mess.

Guest rooms for visiting parents of students are also available. The Hostel complex have other stores for procuring basic commodities such as Soaps, Hairdressing products. The hostels have their respective messes. The students are only allowed to procure food from their respective mess.

All in all, this Mess Managament System will help the students to help them by informing the students in changes in mess system and will also help them to pay the mess charges on time.
